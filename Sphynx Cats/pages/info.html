General Information

What is the personality of a Sphynx Cat

 Sphynx cats get along well with other cats, dogs, and well-behaved children. Sphynx kittens are very energetic and very acrobatic. Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens love to play and show off and can keep a family entertained for hours. Sphynx cats are very intelligent. The Sphynx cat will learn their name very fast and will come when you call them. Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens are so smart they are even able to learn tricks. With more of a dog-like personality, many Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens know how to play fetch!
Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens are also very talkative. Our Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens will talk back to us when we talk to them, it’s almost like you can carry on a conversation with them! The Sphynxes are also very curious cats. Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens do not like to be left out of anything that is going on around them. Many times they will demand to be included in all household activities. If you are working on a project most likely your Sphynx kitten or Sphynx cat will right there with you trying to help out.

Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens are so bare that they are always looking for a warm place. A favorite of the Sphynx cat or Sphynx kitten is right on their owner’s lap. Sphynx cats also like to curl up around their owner’s neck. The Sphynx cat and Sphynx kitten are also known to find their way under a nice warm shirt, while it is being worn! Much like a small dog, Sphynx cats like to sleep in the bed with their owners. So if you’re looking for a cat or kitten that is affectionate, intelligent, and a very good companion, a Sphynx cat or Sphynx kitten might be the one for you.

What colors do Sphynx cats come in?

Sphynx cats can come in any color that a hairy cat can be, but the color of the Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens is on their skin. Sphynx cats can give a variety of colors. Sphynx kittens could be red, blue, black, cream, bi-color, tortoiseshell, calico’s, color points, minks, and also sepia’s. Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens can also have a variety of eye colors including, gold, green, amber, blue and aqua. Looking for a Sphynx kitten keep in mind that pictures do not always give them the justice they deserve. So it is always better to visit a local Sphynx cattery to choose your pet there.

Do Sphynx Cats need special care?



Reputable Sphynx breeders feel it is important to raise their Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens as home pets first. Very often Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens are all a part of the family and are very well socialized. All of them are pretty much pre-spoiled before going to their new homes. Responsible breeders strive daily to provide a clean disease-free environment for their Sphynx cats and Sphynx kittens to grow up in. Because the Sphynx are hairless cats, they must also be raised in a fairly warm environment, especially as young Sphynx kittens.
